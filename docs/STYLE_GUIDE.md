## Lorenz & Son Plumbing Style Guide

### Brand Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `--color-navy` | `#0B1E3F` | Primary backgrounds, headings, buttons |
| `--color-gold` | `#C89B3C` | Accent buttons, borders, highlights |
| `--color-cream` | `#F9F4EA` | Page background, soft sections |
| `--color-slate` | `#1F2A44` | Body text, icons |
| `--color-accent` | `#3F7CAC` | Links, hover states |
| `--color-success` | `#1CA674` | Status badges |

### Typography

- **Headings:** Playfair Display, 600–700 weight, uppercase tracking for subheads.
- **Body:** Source Sans Pro, 400–600 weight, comfortable line-height (1.7).
- **Badges / CTAs:** Uppercase letter spacing `0.25em` minimum.

### Components

- **Buttons**
  - Primary: Gold background, navy text, pill shape, shadow lift on hover.
  - Secondary: Transparent with gold border, invert on hover.
- **Cards:** Rounded 24px corners, cream background, gold border hover accent.
- **Sections:** Alternating cream and white backgrounds with subtle gradients.
- **Sticky Widget:** Navy background, gold CTA, fixed bottom-right with 16px radius.

### Imagery & Iconography

- Use the circular badge logo (`/static/logo.jpg`) on cream or navy backgrounds.
- Plumbing-specific imagery should include fixtures, piping schematics, or tool silhouettes with muted tones.
- Maintain consistent line weight for illustrative icons.

### Voice & Tone

- Confident, technically precise, and reassuring.
- Emphasize response times, certifications, and safety.
- Avoid jargon without explanation; pair technical terms with plain-English context.
